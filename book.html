üß≥ Travel Tips

Avoid overpacking. Use lightweight luggage and pack essentials only.

Arrive at least 2 hours early for domestic flights and 3 hours for international.

Store your passport, visa, boarding pass, and ID in one accessible pouch to avoid last-minute panic.

Flights can dehydrate you quickly. Carry a refillable water bottle and avoid too much caffeine.

Save time and avoid queues by checking in online 24 hours before your flight.

Use a sturdy tag with your name, contact, and destination to identify your luggage easily.

Keep your devices charged during long travel hours with a fully-charged power bank (under airline limits).

Wear loose, breathable clothing and slip-on shoes for security checks and flight comfort.
